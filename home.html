About Us

Gofa Leano Financial Services, also known as K2021162910(SOUTH AFRICA)(PTY)Ltd, is a trusted financial services provider dedicated to offering customized funeral and life insurance solutions. As an authorized financial services provider (FSP No. 53507), we are committed to helping our clients secure their financial future with tailored insurance quotes and efficient claims assistance.

Our experienced team works diligently to understand the unique needs of each client, ensuring that they receive the best possible coverage and support during life's most challenging times.

At Gofa Leano Financial Services, we believe in the importance of planning and preparedness. In addition to our comprehensive insurance offerings, we provide free drafting and consultation services for Wills and Last Testaments.

Our goal is to offer peace of mind to our clients by ensuring that their loved ones are taken care of in the event of the unexpected.

With a client-centric approach and a dedication to excellence, we strive to build lasting relationships and provide exceptional service to all our clients.
